## Description

Clearly is an AI-powered platform that generates professional Business Requirements Documents (BRDs) and Product Requirements Documents (PRDs) through a guided wizard interface. Users answer questions about their project in plain language, and Clearly produces complete, structured documents ready for stakeholders and development teams.

## Problem Solved

Writing BRDs and PRDs manually is slow (days to weeks) and inconsistent. Clearly reduces document creation time to 15-30 minutes while producing higher-quality, more complete requirements.

## How It Works

1. User selects document type (BRD or PRD)
2. AI wizard asks targeted questions about project scope, users, features, and constraints
3. Clearly generates a complete structured document
4. User reviews, edits, and exports (PDF/Word)

- **BRD** — Business Requirements Document (stakeholder-focused, business objectives, scope)

## Technical Details

- Firebase authentication (Google OAuth)
- AI powered by OpenRouter API
- Export: PDF, Word (.docx)
- Collaboration: Real-time with Firebase
