What is Clearly?
Clearly is an AI-powered platform that revolutionizes requirements documentation. Through intelligent conversations, our system learns about your project and generates professional Business Requirements Documents (BRDs) and Product Requirements Documents (PRDs) that would typically take days to create manually.
The platform delivers:

✨ AI conversations that adapt to your expertise level
📄 Automatic generation of professional BRDs, PRDs, and implementation plans
🎯 Dual modes: Plain language for business, technical depth for developers
🔄 Complete version control with one-click regeneration
🚀 Direct integration with AI coding tools (Loveable, V0, Bolt)

How Clearly Works
1. Create Your Project
Sign up and create your project in under a minute. Choose your mode:

Plain Mode: Business-focused, generates code-ready prompts
Technical Mode: Developer-focused, generates detailed task lists

2. BRD Wizard: Define Your Business Requirements
Have an intelligent conversation about:

Project vision and objectives
Target users and stakeholders
Business constraints and success criteria
Scope and expected outcomes

The wizard adapts based on your answers, ensuring comprehensive coverage without unnecessary questions.
3. Generate Your BRD
Click "Generate BRD" and receive a professional document with:

Executive Summary
Business Objectives
Stakeholder Analysis
Scope Definition
Success Metrics
Constraints and Assumptions

Review, regenerate with feedback if needed, or approve to continue.
4. PRD Wizard: Detail Product Requirements
Building on your BRD, explore:

Functional requirements and features
User stories and scenarios
Technical considerations
Integration and security needs

5. Generate Your PRD
Receive a comprehensive Product Requirements Document with:

Detailed feature specifications
User interface requirements
Data and integration needs
Performance requirements
Acceptance criteria

6A. Plain Mode: Get Your Coding Prompt
Automatically receive a production-ready prompt for AI coding tools:

Loveable: Complete web application generation
V0: UI/UX design and prototyping
Bolt: Full-stack development

Copy, paste, and let AI build your application.
6B. Technical Mode: Generate Task List
Receive a detailed implementation plan with:

Prioritized tasks (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
Descriptions and acceptance criteria
Effort estimates and dependencies
Technical tags and component mapping
Sprint-ready breakdown
